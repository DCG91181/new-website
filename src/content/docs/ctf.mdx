## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
<details open>
  <summary style="font-size: 25px">What are Weekly challenges?</summary>
> Weekly challenges are cybersecurity challenges designed to test participants' hacking skills in a controlled, educational environment. Challenges often involve solving puzzles, finding vulnerabilities, and overcoming security obstacles.
</details>

<details>
  <summary style="font-size: 25px">Who can participate in these challenges?</summary>
> Our challenges are open to anyone passionate about cybersecurity! Whether you're a seasoned professional, a curious beginner, or an enthusiast eager to learn, our challenges cater to diverse skill levels.
</details>

<details>
  <summary style="font-size: 25px">How do I submit the flag?</summary>
> - **Join our Discord Server:** Head over to our Discord server and navigate to the designated "submit-flag" channel.
> - **Submit Your Flag:** Use the command `/submit-flag flag{your_flag}` in the channel. Replace `flag{your_flag}` with the flag code you've obtained during the challenge.
> - **Bot Confirmation:** Our Discord bot will automatically verify the flag you've submitted. It will promptly respond, confirming if your submitted flag is correct or incorrect.

> By following these steps, you can efficiently submit your flags and receive immediate feedback from our Discord bot regarding the accuracy of your submission.
</details>

<details>
  <summary style="font-size: 25px">How long will each challenge remain active?</summary>
> The challenge will be active for a duration of one week. Once this period concludes, the challenge will retire, making way for a new and exciting challenge to take its place.
</details>

<details>
  <summary style="font-size: 25px">Are there specific prerequisites or tools required?</summary>
> No specific prerequisites are mandatory. Basic knowledge of cybersecurity concepts may be helpful, and as for tools, we may recommend certain resources, but participation primarily relies on your problem-solving abilities and willingness to learn.
</details>

<details>
  <summary style="font-size: 25px">How can I seek support or report an issue?</summary>
> If you encounter any issues, have queries, or need assistance, please reach out to us using our discord server. We're here to help.
</details>

<details>
  <summary style="font-size: 25px">How often are new challenges released?</summary>
> New challenges are typically released weekly. Stay tuned to our platform or announcements for updates on challenge schedules.
</details>
